關於零時政府

g0v.tw 台灣零時政府從 2012 年 10 月開始，用數位世代的思維，以開源（open source）方式號召群眾以網路世代的新模式協作，運用科技力量促進政府透明化。自 2014 年會之後，無論是中央或是地方政府，甚至媒體與 NGO ，都開始嘗試參與 g0v 或是導入 g0v 開放的工作模式。從急診即時資訊系統、憲餅廚房到線上法規調適平台，都能看到 g0v 跨界之後產生的互動效應。

About g0v.tw

Initiated in October 2012, the community of g0v.tw gathers crowd start from using the thought of digital era by open source method calling crowds to collaborate under new mode of Internet generation, using the power of technology to stimulate government transparency. After 2014 summit, both central and local government, even media and NGOs, are starting to participate in g0v or bringing the openness and work mode of g0v into their organization. From real-time ER information system, Constitution Kitchen, to e-Rulemaking Platforms, you could see the interactive effect of g0v crossing the boundary.
